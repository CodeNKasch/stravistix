Important note. Fitness trend accuracy improved using an heart rate monitor

From now, heart rate based activities have their stress scores processed with Heart Rate Stress Score (HRSS) method. Previously they were calculated using Training Impulse (TRIMP) method. Using HRSS over TRIMP is profitable because it allows direct comparison with others stress scores such as cycling Power Stress Score (PSS) & Swiming Stress Score (SSS) and a right use of the training zones (overload, optimal, neutral, freshness...). In previous version, you were able to use training zones with TRIMPS scored activities: the interpretation of the zones were clearly less accurate. ...
